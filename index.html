I am a senior at Huazhong University of Science and Technology and an incoming MS Robotics student at Carnegie Mellon's Robotics Institute. Previously, I was a research assistant at UC Berkeley, advised by Prof. Masayoshi Tomizuka.

My research focuses on robot learning, where I develop algorithms that enable robots to interact with complex and unfamiliar environments. I'm working towards a future where robots can do anything humans can do, and more.
